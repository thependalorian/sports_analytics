## üí° **KEY INSIGHTS: LESSONS FROM 13 CAMPUSES**

### **Insight 1: Not All Campuses Are Equal**
- **Main Campus (Windhoek):** 40% of students, best infrastructure
- **Regional Campuses:** Vary widely (Oshakati strong, Henties Bay limited)
- **Implication:** Pilot at Main Campus only (focused, manageable), expand selectively

### **Insight 2: Multi-Campus = Unique Differentiator**
- **No competitor offers multi-campus management dashboard**
- **UNAM needs:** Central view + campus-level drill-down
- **Product Feature:** "Organization Hierarchy" (HQ ‚Üí Campuses ‚Üí Teams)
- **Competitive Moat:** Hard for single-sport/single-campus platforms to replicate

### **Insight 3: Geographic Diversity = Product Testing**
- **Windhoek:** Excellent connectivity, test advanced features
- **Rundu/Katima:** Poor connectivity, test offline-first rigorously
- **Oshakati:** Oshiwambo-speaking, test localization
- **Benefit:** If it works across 13 UNAM campuses, it works anywhere in Namibia

### **Insight 4: Institutional Pricing is Key**
- **Per-campus pricing = N$156K/year** (UNAM will reject)
- **Institutional license = N$60K/year** (62% discount, affordable)
- **Psychology:** "One price, unlimited campuses" feels fair and simple
- **Lesson:** Price for value, not per-unit consumption
