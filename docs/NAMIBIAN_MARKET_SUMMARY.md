### **1. Legal & Regulatory Compliance**
üìÑ **Primary Document:** [PRD - Web Dashboard, Section 7.4](PRD_WEB_DASHBOARD.md#74-namibian-regulatory-compliance)

**Regulatory Bodies:**
- ‚úÖ **CRAN** (Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia) - Telecoms, internet, broadcasting
- ‚úÖ **BON** (Bank of Namibia) - Payment systems, mobile money, fintech (Payment Systems Management Act 2003)
- ‚úÖ **NAMFISA** (Namibia Financial Institutions Supervisory Authority) - Insurance, capital markets
- ‚úÖ **NCC** (Namibian Competition Commission) - Anti-competitive practices (Competition Act 2003)
- ‚úÖ **Namibia Gaming Board** - Sports betting, gambling (Gambling Act 2018)

**Legislation:**
- ‚úÖ **Electronic Transactions Act (2019)** - Electronic signatures, consumer protection, cybersecurity
- ‚úÖ **Labour Act (2007)** - Employment contracts, 24 days leave, SSC contributions
- ‚úÖ **Income Tax Act (1981, amended 2023)** - 15% VAT, 32% corporate tax, WHT rules
- ‚úÖ **Data Protection Bill (Pending 2025-2026)** - GDPR-equivalent (proactive compliance implemented)
- ‚úÖ **Contract Law** - Roman-Dutch common law system, force majeure clauses

**Key Compliance Requirements:**
- Data privacy: GDPR-equivalent standards (future-proof)
- Player consent: Adults + parental consent for minors
- Sports betting: 5-second delay for live data, Gaming Board licensing
- Tax: 15% VAT (>N$500K turnover), 32% corporate tax
- Mobile money: KYC/AML (if >N$10M/year, otherwise partner with MTC)
- Video rights: Clear ownership in contracts
- Employment: Written contracts, SSC registration

**Costs:**
- Legal counsel: N$60K/year retainer
- Accounting/audit: N$48K/year
- Insurance: N$42K/year (cyber + E&O + general)
- **Total:** N$150K/year compliance costs

### **2. Infrastructure & Operations**
üìÑ **Primary Document:** [Architecture Documentation](architecture.md#infrastructure-resilience-namibian-context)

**Load Shedding (Power Outages):**
- **Frequency:** 2-3 times per week, 2-4 hours per session
- **MTC Data Center:** UPS (10min) ‚Üí Generator (48hrs) ‚Üí 99.9% uptime
- **Stadium Kits:** Raspberry Pi 5 + 12V battery (8hrs) + solar (N$11,500 per kit)
- **Mobile App:** 6-hour battery target, low-power mode

**Network Connectivity:**
- **MTC:** 60% market share, 5G (Windhoek CBD), 4G (cities), 3G (rural)
- **TN Mobile:** 30% market share, backup provider
- **Failover Chain:** MTC ‚Üí TN Mobile ‚Üí Starlink ‚Üí Offline mode
- **Optimization:** Response compression (80%), connection keep-alive, 30s timeout

**Internet Statistics:**
- Mobile penetration: 108% (2.8M connections)
- Internet penetration: 53% (1.4M users)
- Smartphone adoption: 65% (1.8M smartphones)
- Data costs: N$99/GB (standard), N$19.98/GB (50GB bundle)

**Hardware Specifications:**
- Minimum: Android 8+, 2GB RAM, 500MB app + 5GB offline data
- Popular devices: Samsung A-series, Huawei, Xiaomi (N$2K-N$5K)
- Stadium: Raspberry Pi 5, 4TB SSD, 4G modem, battery backup

**Disaster Recovery:**
- RTO: 4 hours, RPO: 1 hour
- Backups: Windhoek (live) ‚Üí Cape Town (hourly) ‚Üí Cloudflare R2 (daily)
- Testing: Quarterly failover drills

### **9. API Design (Offline-First Sync)**
üìÑ **Primary Document:** [API Documentation](API_DOCUMENTATION.md)

**Namibian API Specifications:**
- **Base URLs:**
  - Production: `https://api.sportsvision.na/api/v1`
  - UNAM Pilot: `https://unam-api.sportsvision.na/api/v1`
  - Staging: `https://staging-api.sportsvision.na/api/v1`
- **Server Location:** MTC Data Center, Windhoek, Namibia
- **CDN:** Cloudflare (global edge caching)

**Network Optimization:**
- Response compression: Gzip/Brotli enabled
- Connection keep-alive: Enabled (reduce 3G latency)
- Timeout: 30s (accommodate 3G)
- Retry logic: 3 attempts, exponential backoff

**Offline-First Endpoints:**
- `/api/v1/sync/matches` - Delta updates (only changed data)
- `/api/v1/sync/players` - Sync player stats
- Conflict resolution: Last-write-wins with timestamp
- Offline queue: Client queues requests for later

**Response Formats:**
- `?compact=true` - 50% smaller (for 3G)
- `?quality=low|medium|high` - Image quality (default: medium)
- `?video_quality=480p|720p|1080p` - Video quality (default: 480p)
- Pagination: Max 50 items (reduce payload)
